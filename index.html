Key Services and Online Opportunities in Kenya

KUCCPS - Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service

KUCCPS places students into universities, colleges, and TVETs after KCSE. It ensures fair placement based on merit and choices and allows for online revision of course preferences.

Visit KUCCPS Portal

HELB - Higher Education Loans Board

HELB provides loans, bursaries, and scholarships to students in tertiary education. It supports tuition, upkeep, and postgraduate studies, with repayment required after graduation.

Visit HELB Website

KRA PIN Registration

A KRA PIN is essential for formal transactions such as employment, opening bank accounts, business registration, and tax filing. It ensures compliance with tax regulations.

Register for KRA PIN

Passport Application

Kenyan citizens can apply for passports online via the eCitizen portal. The system allows for form filling, appointment booking, and tracking application status, reducing delays and promoting transparency.
